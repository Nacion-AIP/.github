# How We Work: Weekly Rhythm

What happens when, and what's expected.

---

## Meeting Schedule

| Day | Time | Meeting | Duration | Purpose |
|-----|------|---------|----------|---------|
| Monday | 9:00 AM | Weekly Planning | 30 min | Define what ships Thursday |
| Mon-Thu | 9:00 AM | Async Update | 2 min | Written status in Slack |
| Mon-Thu | 9:15 AM | Sync Standup | 15 min | Coordinate & unblock |
| Thursday | 4:00 PM | Deploy | 30 min | Ship to production |
| Friday | 10:00 AM | Demo + Retro | 1 hour | Show & improve |

**Total**: ~3 hours/week

---

## Weekly Checklist

### Monday - What ships Thursday?
- [ ] Define the ONE thing we're shipping
- [ ] Break into slices (1-3 days each)
- [ ] Assign owners
- [ ] Schedule pairing sessions

### Tue-Thu - Build & integrate
- [ ] Post standup by 9 AM
- [ ] Merge to main daily
- [ ] Pair on integration points

### Thursday - Ship it
- [ ] All code merged by 3 PM
- [ ] Deploy at 4 PM
- [ ] Feature flags OFF

### Friday - Enable & learn
- [ ] Enable for team, then clients
- [ ] Demo what we shipped
- [ ] One thing to improve next week

---

## Daily Standup (Async + Sync)

### Async Update (by 9 AM in Slack)

```
**[Your Name] - [Date]**

Yesterday: [PR links or "still working on X"]
Today: [What you're shipping/merging]
Need: [Pairing time / review / input - or "nothing"]
Blockers: [Specific issue - or "none"]
Status: [On track / behind / need help]
```

**Keep it short** - 5 bullets max, 2 minutes to write.

### Sync Call (9:15 AM, 15 min)

Not for reading updates. For:
1. Coordinate dependencies
2. Solve blockers
3. Adjust scope if needed
